Apple, Spinach, Onion Quesadillas

Description

These quesadillas put a twist on the traditional recipe, introducing a mix of savory and sweet flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tortillas
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Apple
  • 1/4 lb Fontina Cheese
  • 1 cup Spinach leaves

Directions

  1. Caramelize the onions in butter until browned and soft.
  2. Grate the cheese and slice your apples thin.
  3. Assemble your quesadillas: Apple, Cheese, Onion, then Spinach.
  4. Over medium heat, cook quesadillas until lightly crispy and somewhat browned. Serve with hot sauce and guacamole.
