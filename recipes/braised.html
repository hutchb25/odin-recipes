Braised Chickpeas

Description

This simple dish works as either a main course or a quick and easy side. Roasty tomatoes and healthy chickpeas round out a fantastic plate element.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of chickpeas
  • 1 Can diced tomatoes
  • A lot of garlic
  • Crusty bread or 1 C cooked Rice
  • 2 C of Chard, any variety, or spinach.

Directions

  1. Cook chickpeas over medium high in a drizzle of cooking oil.
  2. Add garlic and tomatoes.
  3. Allow to simmer and thicken; then, add your finely chopped chard.
  4. Serve over cooked rice or next to crusty bread!
